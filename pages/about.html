About Us

The Broken Thorne was built hundreds of years ago by a mysterious man, of whom many legend hath been written. The Inn was passed down through his lineage until it finally found its rest in the hands of Arfin, a friendly though fierce human woman.

Arfin has dedicated her life to creating an environment where adventurers and townsfolk alike will find peace, comfort, and the occasional bar brawl. Arfin lives in the tower, a more recent addition to the historic building.

The Inn has a complete bunkhouse, plus two additional single rooms for patrons. A long tavern runs the length of the building, and during the day it is lit solely from its large windows. The menu is sparse but of great quality.
